Curious about some of the lesser known details of Temuair? If so, you've come to the right place! Here is a collection of random uncommon knowledge


- Groups with all 5 classes get bonus EXP when hunting
- Increased INT stats will reduce the spell damage you take
- The Monk Mantis Kick skill will do more damage if there are Aislings around / ontop of you. Stacking Aislings can make it quite strong.
