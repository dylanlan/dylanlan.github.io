The Inventory Display


By clicking on the inventory icon you can look at what you are currenty carrying. You can also drop, use, or give away items. When you click on the inventory icon, the main window will display items currenty in your inventory:




Picking up and dropping items
To pick an item up off the ground, double click it. It will then appear in your inventory. If you click and drag an item from your inventory to the scene window, or onto another Aisling, you will drop or give the item away.

Identifying items
If you move your cursor over an item in your inventory, its name will appear in the information bar underneath the item window. If the description is not very informative, you might try to identify the item, which is a skill that many of the classes possess.

Dropping or giving away gold
If you click and drag the pile of gold into the scene window or onto another Aisling, a window will pop up asking how much gold you want to give away.

Using or equipping items
If the item is a consumable such as wine or a blue potion, double clicking on it will allow you to use the item. If the item is equipment in the form of a necklace, armor or a weapon, double clicking the item will allow you to equip it. If you cannot equip it, the reason will be displayed in the message bar. Equipping and removing items is discussed in more detail in the Miscellaneous section.
