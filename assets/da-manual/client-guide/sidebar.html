The Sidebar


The sidebar is the home of the picture window. You can upload a portrait that will be displayed in the picture window to other Aislings. To learn more, click here. To see other Aislings' pictures, click on them; the picture will be displayed in the picture box. Underneath that is your name, in case you forgot, and a sun dial, which keeps track of night and day in Temuair.

Quick Tips on Pictures
Pick a picture that is appropriate to your character. Your graphics should be "in-character", meaning that pictures from other games, etc, are inappropriate. Pick something that represents who you are, and something that you feel comfortable representing you.
