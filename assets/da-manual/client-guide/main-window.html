The Main Window




This window is the main interface for Dark Ages. Its contents change with regards to the icons, but a brief discussion of its purpose is below.

Main Window

The main window's contents will change based on what icon you click. There are five icons in all, and they are discussed in more detail in the next sections.

Message Bar

Messages will appear in this bar. Server messages, such as "Chaos is rising up" will appear, as well as messages from other Aislings. In addition, when you enter the game, the time of date will appear here.

Information / Typing Bar

When you are talking to other Aislings, what you type will appear here. When you are looking at your inventory or spells, the identification will appear here as well.
