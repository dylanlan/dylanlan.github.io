The Spells Display


By clicking on the spells icon you can see what skills you have learned. You can also use these skills on items or other Aislings. The main window will change to show you the skills currently available to you.




Learning spells
To learn spells, visit your local master. Wizards, for instance, can visit the wizard masters in different cities (Dar in Mileth, for example) to learn various elemental spells, whereas priests can visit Bertil to learn the healing arts.

Using spells
If the spell affects an Aisling, you can use it in one of two ways: by double clicking on the spell icon, and then clicking on the Aisling you wish to cast it on; or by dragging the spell icon onto the Aisling you wish to cast it on. You can cast combat spells on monsters in the same manner. Take note that you cannot cast offensive spells while in any neutral area (towns and entrances to monster lairs)

Getting rid of a spell
If you ever desire to forget a spel you have learned, visit any master of the arts anywhere in Temuair (Dar, for example). All of the masters will help you forget a spell that you no longer want knowledge of.
